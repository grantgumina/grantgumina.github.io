about

We're a team of engineering students from Purdue University who think they've got the next big idea. It's called Moments.

We've taken the conventional priorities and paradigms for a social app and turned them on their head, reworking them from the ground up. This process has culminated in what we think is going to be a great user experience.

Right now we're still in an alpha stage, but are quickly approaching a public beta. If you're at all interested in helping us build the next big thing, sign up below to get notified when Moments ships. Thank you.
